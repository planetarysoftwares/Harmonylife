Our funeral Plan

As we grow older, we start to think about what will happen and how our loved ones will cope after we’ve gone. Many people would like to put their mind at rest by leaving something towards funeral expenses, unpaid bills or to leave a nest egg for loved ones. Our funeral Plan takes care of all.

At Harmony life we help you to prepare for the unexpected, We ensure that you and your loved ones are buried with dignity despite your financial situation and those left behind are well taken care of, leaving you with comfort and a peace of mind...because We Care For You.

Our Benefit Solution includes:
